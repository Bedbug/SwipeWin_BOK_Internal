  1. How the Competition Works

    1. Employees must log into the “Bok Trivia” platform using their oracle/staff number either on their phone or web via the Competition link.

    2. Each round of the Competition comprises of 15 questions and the users have 5 minutes to answer them all.

    3. When the 5 minutes is over the round is over, users receive points upon answering questions correctly.

    4. Points are awarded as follows:

      1. Twenty (20) points if this is a “common” type question;

      2. Fifty (50) points if this is a “bronze” type question;

      3. One hundred points (100) if this is a “silver” type question; and

      4. One hundred and fifty points (150) if this is a “golden” type question.

    5. Bronze, silver and gold questions award extra points and pop up at surprise intervals.



  1. Prizes

    1. Play one (1) of the three (3) rounds of 15 questions each week, collect at least one (1) point to stand a chance to win.

    2. All weekly entrants with at least one (1) point will go into a random lucky draw conducted on MTN Office Apps in order to draw the three (3) winners for that week. Winners stand to win these weekly prizes:

      1. R1000 (one thousand Rands) in MoMo cash.

      2. A years’ subscription to Bok Squad* (herein referred to as the Prize/s).

*On the Bok Squad platform, MTNers cannot participate or win in any of the competitions open to the public, but have access to:

1. Behind the scenes Springbok footage

2. Exclusive player interviews

3. Video content

4. Purchasing early bird tickets for the 2023 Rugby World Cup
